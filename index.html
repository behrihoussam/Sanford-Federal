WHO WE ARE

Our mission is to empower our employees to develop solutions that break through barriers – making technology smarter, communication easier, nations safer, and communities stronger.

Sanford Federal INC. Headquartered In Stafford, VA Is a premier, federal professional services company, primarily engaged in providing advanced information technology, cyber security, management systems support, as well as business and infrastructure management services. We are among the most innovative, respected and ethical providers of technology solutions to the United States government. Our mission is to provide superior-quality, innovative, information technology solutions that help federal agencies best aid, serve and protect the American people.

OUR SERVICES

Logistics And Acquisition Support

Sanford Federal Inc. International is a global operation that is ready to meet the needs of the government, whether they are in the continental United States or halfway around the world. From acquisition and material readiness to total supply chain management, our team is able to deploy and support the United States government and its mission, wherever it may be. Whether we are delivering fuel to our troops overseas and provisioning for and managing the flow of supplies in dangerous and volatile conflict zones around the world, or managing logistics for the Department of Defense stateside, we stand ready to assist the vast needs of the federal government. We are able to deliver a wide range of logistics support to our clients to ensure mission performance, wherever they may be.

Cybersecurity

Sanford Federal understands the risks of operating in an increasingly connected world. Our team leverages our expertise in cybersecurity to provide the U.S. Government with the tools it needs to keep our country safe from foreign and domestic threats. Sanford Federal INC. developed a holistic, integrated approach to both offensive and defensive cybersecurity. This approach encompasses legacy, prevailing and emerging technologies, architectures, risk management, policies, situational awareness, and intelligence. Utilizing the most advanced tools available, our seasoned team of accomplished industry experts provide our clients with our unique and unparalleled set of capabilities in order to solve their most difficult cybersecurity challenges.

Data Analysis And Management

Our team provides the federal government with solutions to its needs in data analysis and management. We integrate legacy, current, and emerging data storage technologies with a variety of content management systems and the latest security. When it comes to understanding metadata management and data warehousing, whether it is on-premise or cloud storage, Sanford Federal Inc. is equipped to handle the data needs of our clients both efficiently and securely.

Healthcare Services

We provide professional healthcare services solutions and innovative products to the US government at home and abroad. Our range of capabilities from professional staffing and augmentation of healthcare personnel to the provision of programs that facilitate clinical development and direct quality patient-centric care allow us to assist our customers in revolutionizing the administration of quality healthcare. Our state-of-the-art technology solutions equip healthcare professionals with innovative electronic network capabilities that enable them to integrate business operations, advance clinical intelligence, implement successful management practices, and ultimately improve the quality and delivery of patient care.

Managed Services

Our team understands the challenges associated with operating large agencies with limited budgets. In order to allow agencies to focus on their missions instead of their growing costs, SANFORD FEDERAL Inc. International provides our clients with outstanding managed services. From IT services, support, and human resources and payroll to project management and supply chain managed services, our team is equipped to augment your agency’s operations and improve productivity. Sanford Federal Inc. ensures that transitions are both seamless and affordable. By leveraging its diverse network of industry-leading partners, Sanford Federal Inc. is able to provide virtually any managed service required.

Information Technology

At Sanford Federal Inc., we recognize the growing requirement of information technology and management. We provide our clients with a suite of full life cycle capabilities in IT, from the design and development phases to installation and evaluation. Our team offers a myriad of capabilities, including but not limited to: network and server administration, infrastructure support, software development, cloud computing and virtualization, interface and web development, modernization, identity and access management, license management, help desk support, and quality assurance. Our talented team of world-class IT professionals is able to provide virtually any IT solution required by our clients.
